## Intended Audience

This Homelab wiki is designed for individuals passionate about building a Homelab with automation at its core. If you envision your Homelab as cattle: resources that can be swiftly provisioned, scaled, and decommissioned as needed, then you're in the right place. Whether you're a seasoned enthusiast or just diving into the world of Homelabs, this Wiki aims to empower you with the knowledge and tools to architect, deploy, and manage your Homelab infrastructure efficiently.
